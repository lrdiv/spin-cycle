Spin Cycle

What is Spin Cycle?

Spin Cycle is a simple service that sends you a daily email at 9am (Central) recommending a random album from your Discogs collection.

We make sure to never recommend the same record twice, allowing you to listen to your entire collection, one day at a time. You can also mark a recommendation as "Not Played" to add it back into the mix for future recommendations.

How Do I Join?

  1. 1. Login via Discogs
  2. 2. Tell us your email address, so we can send your recommendations
  3. 3. That's it! You'll get a daily email with a record from your collection
